* Tech Roast
* Tech Panel
* Tech Dance Party with Music and other performances
* Street Interviews
* Tech Couch
* Newsbits
* Lab
* Tech Dinner
* Code and Learn
* Hack Collaborations
* Co-working
* and any other creative tech initiative
